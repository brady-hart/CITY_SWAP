1. Type city names. Type a city name from memory to unlock a pin. Large pins are scoring pins. They are numbered 1-7 (based on population size). Example: Chicago is the largest city in the region of Illinois, so it will be pin number 1. It will also have a random color.
2. Swap for the right color. Swap pins by number. A blue #1 can be swapped for a yellow #1 (see above). And so on. Swap until all 7 scoring pins inside a region are the same color. That will clear the region. Once you've cleared 5 regions, you win! Note that some regions have less than 7 scoring pins. These regions are not clearable.
3. Identify small pins to find items. Small pins have a chance of being an "Inspector pin" or a "Wild pin". These item pins can be played on scoring pins. Inspector pins reveal the name of an unidentified pin. Wild pins change the color of any scoring pin to rainbow. Rainbow pins count as any color.
